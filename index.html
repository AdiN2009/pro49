Interesting Facts about bears

  • Bears can smell food, cubs, a mate or predators from miles away.
  • Canada is home to nearly 60 % of the world's polar bears.
  • Bears have two layers of fur. A short layer of fur keeps the bear warm and a long layer keeps water away from the skin and short fur.

Interesting Facts about cicadas

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.
  • Some cicadas could explode with fungus.
  • Cicadas can't eat so they suck fluids and nutrients from tree roots.

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels can go on for weeks without drinking water.
  • Camels can live for about 40 years.
  • Camels can run 65km/h in short bursts.
